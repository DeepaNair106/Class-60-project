DescriptionGeorges Prosper Remi, known by the pen name Hergé, was a Belgian cartoonist. He is best known for creating The Adventures of Tintin, the series of comic albums which are considered one of the most popular European comics of the 20th century.

He was born on 22 May 1907 at Etterbeek, Belgium

He died on 3 March 1983 at Woluwe-Saint-Lambert, Belgium
